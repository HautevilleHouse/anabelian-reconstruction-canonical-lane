# Geometric Galois Bridge

This note records the remainder rule and the mainstream bridge vocabulary for the Anabelian Reconstruction workspace.

Write the ambient class as `X = X_mc ⊎ R`, where `X_mc` is the manifold-constrained admissible sector, `P_mc X = X_mc`, and `Q_rem = I - P_mc` records the explicit remainder.

The public strengthening rule is:

- closure on `X_mc` does not identify the full reconstruction problem with the closed lane,
- any claim extending from the admissible reconstruction sector to full geometric reconstruction must retain the remainder ledger,
- the remainder cannot be silently erased by replacing a projected reconstruction datum with the whole object.

Mainstream bridge vocabulary for this repo includes etale fundamental groups, exact sequences to absolute Galois groups, outer Galois actions, decomposition and inertia data where relevant, and reconstruction of the underlying geometric object from these invariants. The note exists to keep the bridge explicit rather than folkloric.
